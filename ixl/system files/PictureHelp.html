Import Pictures from Computer

1. Navigate to the picture that is stored on your computer.

2. Select a picture. You can use the Control and the Shift Keys to select multiple picture files simultaneously.

3. Copy the picture(s) into My Library. Once the handheld is connected and turned on, transfer the pictures from My Library to My Handheld.

Notes:

- If your pictures are stored on an online site, then you need to copy your photos to your computer first, and then transfer them to the iXL handheld.

- Only .JPEG pictures can be imported.
