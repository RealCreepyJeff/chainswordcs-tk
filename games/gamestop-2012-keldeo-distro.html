Pokemon Black & White 2012 Keldeo Distribution Cart

This is the first Not-For-Resale DS cartridge I ever got, a couple years ago (as of 2019) for $35 or so on eBay. This distribution was to generate hype for the upcoming release of Pokemon Black 2 and White 2, and it included a Keldeo that the player could trade to the new games when they came out.
There's not much more to say that hasn't been said, so I'll link to some good websites for further info. I decided to take a crack at recreating the advertisement poster used by GameStop for the event. That poster can be found here. I included blurred and non-blurred versions, because some of the text got kinda pixelated.
